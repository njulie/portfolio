Design Process: Urban Styles Website

Goal: Give Urban Styles contemporary dance group an online presence for publicity, general information, and contact.

Channeling Inspiration

Urban Styles is a contemporary dance group that possesses characteristics of a refined company as well as the sassy jazz side of funk and fun. I wanted my moodboard to capture fun, boldness, and elegance. Inspiration came from geometric shapes, primary colors, and minimalistic, simple designs.

Going For Quantity

Narrowing In

From my preliminary sketches, I selected a few ideas that were unique to expand upon. How unique and out there can I get with this? I kept my wireframes rough and quick by prototyping in Keynote.

Experimenting with Typography

I ultimately settled on A because the more rigid, sharp-angled typeface of "URBAN" looks strong and structured, while the script typeface emulates the artistry and style of contemporary movement.
