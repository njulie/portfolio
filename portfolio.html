Design Process: My Online Portfolio

Goal: Design an online portfolio to showcase your past projects and work for future employers to view.

Initial Moodboard

I wanted to capture a sense of vibrancy while maintaining professionalism in my portfolio, so I drew inspiration from structured elements and color. I particularly liked gridded elements to represent structure, and I wanted to express the unstructured nature of my personality through typography.
