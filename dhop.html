Design Process: DHOP Event Flyer

Goal: In four days, design a flyer to publicize TriDelta Sorority's Delta House of Pancakes, a fundraising event to benefit St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, where members sell pancakes on Sigma Chi fraternity's front lawn.


Must abide by the following restrictions:

  • Blue and gold color scheme
  • Mention Tridelta and St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
  • Date, time, location of event
  • Unlimited pancakes!
  • Ticket prices

Transferring Mind to Paper

I began to play around with various ways to display and arrange information on a hierarchy based on which data was most important.

Two Heads Are Better Than One

At this point, I was working with another member of the organization, so I sent her my initial sketches for her to create a rough draft of a potential flyer. We both liked the sketched design with the event info displayed in three triangles and the sketched design with the pancake intersecting the top half of the flyer and bottom half.

From this design, I kept the phrasing and information, and refined the color scheme, typography, and placement of triangles for a less symmetrical look for a modern and fresh feel in the next iteration.
