Design Event Flyer (TriDelta DHOP)

Challenge: In four days, design a flyer to publicize TriDelta Sorority's Delta House of Pancakes, a fundraising event to benefit St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.


Must abide by the following restrictions:

  • Blue and gold color scheme
  • Mention Tridelta and St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
  • Date, time, location of event
  • Unlimited pancakes!
  • Ticket prices

Transferring Mind to Paper

Here's me playing around with various ways to display and arrange information based on what was most important.

Two Heads Are Better Than One

Working with another sorority member, we decided to combine the first design (top left in JPG 1) with the fifth design (top right in JPG 2) because we liked them both. I kept the phrasing and information but refined the color scheme and typography. I also placed the triangles in a jagged alignment so it looked less rigid and more modern.

Three Variations on a Theme

Of the three variations, I decided to select variation 1. Version 3 fonts gave off too much of an old diner vibe, with the serifs of "DHOP". Version 2 was better, but the script font was a bit too much fancy, and our event (classy as we are), didn't possess the elegance that the cursive font embodied. Variation 1 was the perfect mix of fun, modern, and simple. And there we go!
