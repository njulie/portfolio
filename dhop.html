Design Process: DHOP Event Flyer

Goal: In four days, design a flyer to publicize TriDelta Sorority's Delta House of Pancakes, a fundraising event to benefit St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, where members sell pancakes on Sigma Chi fraternity's front lawn.


Must abide by the following restrictions:

  • Blue and gold color scheme
  • Mention Tridelta and St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
  • Date, time, location of event
  • Unlimited pancakes!
  • Ticket prices

Transferring Mind to Paper

I began to play around with various ways to display and arrange information on a hierarchy based on which data was most important.
